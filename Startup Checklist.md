# Freedom Bowls Startup Checklist

Date: May 19, 2026

This is the working launch checklist for Freedom Bowls. The editable version also lives in the app under **Startup Checklist** and is backed by Supabase.

## Planning

- Confirm business name, brand, and menu direction
- Decide trailer versus step van
- Get written ballpark quotes for truck, hood, fire suppression, electrical, and plumbing
- Build a final startup budget with included, alternative, optional, and reserve items

## Compliance

- Confirm mobile food unit plan review requirements with the local health department
- Confirm fire marshal requirements for hood and suppression
- Secure commissary or approved base of operations
- Confirm business registration, sales tax, and employer tax setup
- Get insurance quotes for vehicle, general liability, and workers comp if needed

## Buildout

- Choose and purchase truck or trailer only after plan review feedback
- Order and install cooking, refrigeration, plumbing, and electrical equipment
- Set up POS, menu boards, packaging, and safety equipment

## Menu

- Finalize core bowls, proteins, sauces, and pricing
- Test recipes at 50, 100, and 200 serving scale

## Launch

- Run a paid test event or soft opening
- Create a catering packet and event quote template
- Book first public events and one or two anchor private accounts
- Set reorder pars and opening inventory levels
- Prepare a CPA handoff packet with startup cost and checklist exports

## Notes

- Keep invoices, titles, permits, inspection approvals, and placed-in-service dates together.
- Mark blocked items in the app whenever a permit, quote, or inspection is still pending.
